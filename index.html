THE STORY


Many lifetimes after the end of civilization, you, "The Organism", awake from your long sleep. The island you will find yourself exploring will be host to many discoveries dark and twisted, as you follow the echoes of the island and its history to carve the path of your ultimate destiny.

You were made for this.


 THE WORLD
    [ Return to Top ]


The world of Deicidium is lush and beautiful, but danger awaits any inhabitant that does not remain constantly vigilant of their surroundings. Caverns, mountains, and natural formations - all will stand between you and your destination, but beware more the structures of unnatural origin and what they bring with them.



Graphics

The island of Deicidium is portrayed with cutting-edge graphical fidelity, with detailed pre-rendered environments and Full-motion Video sequences.

  Minimum System Requirements for Windows 95 and MS-DOS

  • 100% IBM PC-compatible computer
  • Quad-speed (4X) CD-ROM drive (600K/ second sustained transfer rate)
  • 35 MB of uncompressed hard disk space
  • 256-colors SVGA 640 x 480 graphics with a VESA local bus (VLB) or PCI video card
  • 100% Sound Blaster 16-compatible sound card (for digital and general MIDI audio) Note: 8-bit sound cards are not supported (e.g., Reveal SC400, Sound Blaster original and Sound Blaster Pro)
